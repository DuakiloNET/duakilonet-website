Hi, I'm **Heri** — a developer who writes about things I learn.

`duakilo.net` is my corner of the internet. No brand, no content strategy. Just a place to think out loud about tech, coding, tools, and the occasional thing I break along the way.

## What I Write About

- **Tools & workflows** — setups that actually improved my day-to-day
- **Tutorials** — things I wish had better documentation
- **Comparisons** — honest technical evaluations, not marketing
- **Lessons learned** — from projects that went well and ones that didn't

## Principles

- No fluff. No listicles.
- Quality over cadence.
- Code examples should be copy-pasteable and tested.
